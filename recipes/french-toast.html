French Toast

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp of freshly grated nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 3/4 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 6 - 8 Slices of bread

Directions

In a shallow large bowl or pie dish, whisk together the whole eggs, egg yolks, cinnamon, nutmeg, sugar, and salt thoroughly.

Whisk in the milk and heavy whipping cream in a slow and steady stream until you have a homogeneous texture.

In batches, add the bread to the liquid and soak on each side for 10 seconds.
